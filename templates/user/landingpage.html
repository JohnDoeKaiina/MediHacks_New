Features the platform provide

QR Code Medical Profile

Users can securely input their medical information and emergency contacts into the app. The app generates a unique QR code that contains the user's medical history, allergies, medications, and emergency contact details.

AI Assistant 911 Operator Help

MedAssist integrates an AI-powered virtual assistant that acts as a preliminary 911 operator. Users can communicate their emergency situation to the AI assistant, which can provide immediate medical advice, CPR instructions, or other critical information.

One-Click Emergency Notification

In case of an accident or emergency, users can trigger an immediate alert to their pre-saved emergency contacts with a single click.

QR Code for Doctors

Medical professionals and emergency responders can scan the user's QR code to instantly access their complete medical profile.

AI companionship during recovery

AI companionship during recovery aiming to enhance emergency response efficiency, ensure timely medical care.

Daily medicine reminders

Users can set customized reminders for each medication they need to take daily. This includes specifying the dosage, frequency, and timing of each medication regimen.
